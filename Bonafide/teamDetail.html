Samir Mahmudov is a managing partner with the firm. Prior to joining BONAFIDE, Samir worked on several senior legal positions, including Of Counsel at OMNI Law Firm, a senior prosecutor at Anti-Corruption Directorate under the Prosecutor General of the Republic of Azerbaijan, etc. 

Samir represents major international and local corporations in various types of commercial transactions, including real estate transactions, loans, sales, secured transactions, carriage of goods and passengers, licensing and distribution agreements. He regularly advises on various aspects of Azerbaijani corporate law (including corporate finance, corporate governance, M&A, etc.), securities and commercial law. In addition, Samir is an experienced litigator and routinely represents clients in civil and criminal litigations.

Samir is a member of a Collegium of Advocates and is permitted to represent clients in all court proceedings, including in the Supreme Court and Constitutional Court.

Starting from 2016, Samir has been constantly teaching core legal subjects, such as Civil Law, Civil Procedure, Corporate Law, International Business Transactions and Law of Obligations at the leading Azerbaijani universities (Baku State University and ADA University).

Samir holds bachelor’s and master’s degrees in law from Baku State University and completed his LLM studies at Syracuse University College of Law.
