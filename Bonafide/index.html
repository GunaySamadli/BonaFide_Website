About

BONAFIDE Law Firm was established in 2018 and despite being a relatively young law firm, has plenty of practice in handling complex legal matters in multiple areas of the economy. Our team of trained professionals takes pride in providing personalized legal service. Our clients include local and foreign companies and organizations, operating in a variety of fields, including aviation, carriage of goods, telecommunications, banking, construction, energy and natural resources, agriculture, etc. Moreover, BONAFIDE Law Firm regularly provides high-quality legal services to Azerbaijani state bodies, public legal entities, and foreign NGOs on various projects of state importance. We enjoy a culture of long-standing client relationships built on trust and anchored in the quality of our service.
